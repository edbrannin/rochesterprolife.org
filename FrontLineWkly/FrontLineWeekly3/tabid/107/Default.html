Saturday, June 14, 2008 in front of Planned Parenthood

The 'Lambs of Christ' were out in front of Planned Parenthood praying the Rosary, for the ending of the baby killing that is going on in our Nation & around the World & for those that are doing this abominable act against God & His children.  There was a good turn out of faithful Catholics witnessing their Faith in the community.  They are not hiding their light under a bushel basket.  Public witnessing is what is needed to end ABORTION.  I gave to Mary Quinn the City Newspaper, of the Carol Love article & the filers of the Pro Life Mass on next Saturday, June 21st at 12 noon which will be celebrated by Fr. John Reif, a devoted priest in the Lord's service. She said that she would bring some of the filers to The Church of the Holy Spirit, in Webster.  Thanks Mary and the Lambs of Christ for all that you do for God's little ones.  

I've noticed that some of the workers that we witnessed to over the years are no longer driving out of the parking lot.  One young woman Michelle is a Catholic from St. Paul's parish, & the other woman Debbie is a ,so called Christian she professes, who once said to me that she worked in the "clean section".  No such area in Planned Parenthood, sorry Debbie that you are deceiving yourself.  I don't know if they have left & have repented or if they just got another job & still are in the mind set that it is okay to murder God's children by Abortion & Birth Control?  We're praying for you Michelle & Debbie.  Michelle might have had a baby & if she did & quite her job to take care of her child.  I only hope & pray that she will reflect on her actions at Planned Parenthood, over the many years that she worked there. She acted as a 'Death Escort' at one time & also gave donations of $ to Planned Parenthood according to past Annual Reports of Planned Parenthood that I received.  She was also a  'Rape Counselor' there at Planned Parenthood & testified against me in Federal Court, back in 1999 in Buffalo, NY, where PP wanted us pro lifers to be in a 'Buffer Zone' in front of their building.  I forgive you Michelle for you know not what you do. 

Over the years many staff people have left Planned Parenthood's 'Killing Mill'.  I pray that you all have "REPENTED" and are living a life in Christ Jesus.  For those who have died, over the years, I only hope & pray that before you took your final breathe that you all 'REPENTED'. 

 

A car stopped, on the street, & asked Martha & I for directions.  The black woman driver said to us that she was pregnant with twins, a boy & a girl.  She then said that Planned Parenthood wanted her to have an abortion.  She said that she almost did but changed her mind.  I said to her back "don't let your babies hear that".  She smiled.  Yes, the baby the womb can & does pick up on the emotions of the mother, either her stress or her calmness.  What doctors are finding out now about what the baby experiences in their mother's wombs in extraordinary.  She then said I have my four other children with me & then the back window came down & there were 3 beautiful people in the back of the van.  The other young adult was in the front of the van, next to her mother.  They all looked in their teens.  Martha then said to the mom, that we have baby clothes at the Focus Pregnancy Help Center & for her to drive in the parking lot a little ways down the street & receive some.  Martha then walked to the center & blessed the family with the beautiful baby clothes & items from which we received on Wednesday by Don. Thanks again Don & I will request more baby clothes on Monday.  She also took a Truth packet & the other literature that we have in the center.  She said that she especially wanted some on Chastity & Marriage & Abstinence because one of her children will be going off to college soon.  Great Mom!!   She was so happy & grateful & we were happy too.  We will be calling her in the near future with some more things for her twins. 

Martha & I stayed out on the Front Lines until the last man & woman was gone out of the Planned Parenthood parking lot.  Amen sister.  Then we went into the center to do some more organizing of baby clothes & literature.

We later saw the DVD  'A Distant Thunder' which is highly recommended by Fr. Frank Pavone from the Priests for Life.   It was on loan from Pat, one of the Lambs of Christ.  Thank you Pat it was an intense movie that both Martha & I will remember for a long time.
