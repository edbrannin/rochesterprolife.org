The Focus Pregnancy Help Center is a non-profit 501(C)3 emergency pregnancy service established by RochesterProLife.org in 2005.  Its mission is to provide meaningful and compassionate care. The Center helps women, men, and families by meeting the various and many practical needs they encounter in a crisis pregnancy including free resource counseling and referrals in the community for assistance.  The Center connects clients to life affirming help and support services.

 

The Focus Pregnancy Help Center offers a safe, non-judgmental haven for those who are distressed by their pregnancy. The Center also provides free pregnancy testing, referrals for free ultrasounds, informational brochures and counseling to all women and men. The Center offers free baby and children clothes and small items and diapers and baby wipes, non-perishable food items and referrals to the vast network of support services available within and outside the Rochester area.
