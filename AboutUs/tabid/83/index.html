Mission Statement of the Focus Pregnancy Help Center

To appropriately face the reality of abortion. To respond with urgency to the immediate need to save a life. To counsel through verbal interaction, literature, and photographic images. Our mission is to provide a life-giving solution to all women & girls contemplating abortion. To help them to choose life and to supply the support & referrals that they need with love.

RochesterProLife.org give thanks to Our Lord

The FOCUS Pregnancy Help Center is a non-profit 501(c)3 emergency pregnancy service established by RochesterProLife.org in 2005. Its mission is to provide meaningful and compassionate care. The Center helps women, men, and families by meeting the various and many practical needs they encounter in a crisis pregnancy including free resource counseling and referrals in the community for assistance. The Center connects clients to life affirming help and support services.

The FOCUS Pregnancy Help Center offers a safe, non-judgmental haven for those who are distressed by their pregnancy. The Center also provides free pregnancy testing, informational brochures and assistance to all women & men. The Center offers referrals to the vast network of support services available within the Rochester area.

  • THE SIDEWALK COUNSELING MINISRTY IS

To honor Almighty God by protecting the defenseless, voiceless and precious pre-born children that He created, and to offer their mothers help to continue their pregnancies. To respect and defend the dignity of the person in the womb.


We are present at Planned Parenthood

@ 114 University Ave to:

 

  • Pray (Rosary, Chaplet of Mercy & Personal Prayers)

 

  • Peacefully protest, carrying signs with words and pictures

 

  • Sidewalk counseling (offering pro-life literature, rosaries, speaking out in offering help to those who are entering and leaving the abortion clinic)

 

  • Educate the public about abortion through discussion and literature

 

  • Call the pro-death advocates to repentance. We love them. We hate what they are doing.

 

Our hope is that by the grace of God, women will change their minds and leave the abortion clinic still pregnant. If a mother does abort her child, we want her child to know how much she or he was loved by us who tried to save their life, and to hold out the hope of repentance and post-abortive counseling to the mother.
